MISSION STATEMENT

Tech Team strives to help others by providing technological assistance, working with nonprofits, and making a positive impact in the local community.

>

WHAT WE DO

Tech Team cultivates a supportive environment to provide access and knowledge with technology through teaching programming, raising money to provide electronics, and creating instructional videos.

ABOUT US

When Tech Team was first started we went around and helped teachers and students with their technology troubles. Recently we have decided to continue with this, but instead of helping hands-on, we are creating videos so we can help more people at a time.
